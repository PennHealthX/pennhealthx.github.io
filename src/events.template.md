# Upcoming Events

**:hamburger: Lunch Talk with Dr. Peter Clardy, Director of Clinical Enterprise at Google for Health | :round_pushpin: Wednesday, 5/20/2026 at 1pm, 516EW**

Dr. Pete Clardy is a pulmonary and critical care physician who directs the Clinical Enterprise Team at Google. He leads a global team of clinicians who work closely with Google’s DeepMind, Research, and Cloud organizations to develop, evaluate and scale solutions that empower providers to organize biomedical information, accelerate scientific discovery, simplify clinical workflows, and improve patient outcomes. He was instrumental in the development and deployment of Google’s clinician-facing search and summarization tools and is currently platforming Google’s most advanced generative AI models for use across the health and life science sector.

Prior to joining Google, Dr. Clardy built a distinguished career in academic medicine, holding numerous leadership positions at Harvard Medical School and its affiliated teaching hospitals. His roles included Director of Medical Intensive Care at Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center, Program Director for the Harvard Pulmonary and Critical Care Fellowship, and Associate Dean for Medical Education at Harvard Medical School. 

An honors graduate of Yale College and the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons, he is a recognized leader in health professional education and serves as an advisor on the impact of AI in the field to the National Academy of Medicine, the Josiah Macy Foundation and the American Board of Internal Medicine. Dr. Clardy maintained his clinical practice as an intensivist in the medical and surgical ICUs through the COVID-19 pandemic until 2022, bringing a deep, firsthand understanding of frontline clinical challenges to his work in medical technology.
