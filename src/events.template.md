# Upcoming Events

**:blue_book: VC and Healthcare Seminar with Dr. Andrew Parambath | :round_pushpin: Monday, June 8th at 12pm, 505EW**

Dr. Parambath completed his MD/MBA at Penn and Wharton before going on to serve as an investor at Pear VC, one of the leading early-stage venture firms in healthcare and technology. He is also currently an EM resident at Stanford.

He will share his journey from medical school into venture capital and offer a broad view of today's healthcare investment landscape, including emerging startups, therapeutic areas, and technologies to watch. Whether you're curious about healthcare entrepreneurship, startup investing, or careers at the intersection of medicine and business, this is a great opportunity to learn from someone who has navigated that path firsthand. Andrew will be joining us in person, and lunch will be provided!

**:hamburger: Lunch Talk with Dr. Kevin Johnson (VP for Applied Clinical Informatics at Penn Medicine) | :round_pushpin: Thursday, June 14th at 12:30pm, 516EW**

Dr. Johnson will describe The Observer Project, which includes the creation of a multimodal repository of real primary care encounters. He will present early findings from AI tools built on that data to improve pre-visit preparation, documentation, agenda management, and cognitive screening.

Kevin B. Johnson, MD, MS is the David L. Cohen University Professor of Biomedical Informatics, Computer Science, Pediatrics, and Science Communication at the University of Pennsylvania, and Vice President for Applied Clinical Informatics at Penn Medicine. He earned his MD from Johns Hopkins University and an MS in Medical Informatics from Stanford University. Prior to joining Penn, Dr. Johnson served as Chair of Biomedical Informatics and Chief Informatics Officer at Vanderbilt University Medical Center. At Penn, he directs the Artificial Intelligence for Ambulatory Care Innovation (AI4AI) lab, advancing AI to reimagine the clinical encounter.
