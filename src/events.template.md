# Upcoming Events

Dr. Sherman Leung | Monday 4/6 12:30pm 506EW
Sherman Leung, MD is a practicing emergency physician in residency at Stanford. He also supports the investing team and a dedicated portfolio of healthcare companies as a venture fellow at a16z bio+health, where he focuses on the intersection of AI and healthcare and care delivery model innovation. Previously as a medical student at the Icahn School of Medicine, Sherman helped launch AlleyCorp Healthcare, a physician-led venture capital firm and venture studio where he led investments behind tech-enabled specialty care models and incubated healthcare companies focused on primary care and value-based care enablement. He also founded MD+, a 501c3 non-profit which now supports over 3000 aspiring physician-innovators and medical students across the country. Before transitioning into medicine, Sherman graduated from Stanford with a BS in Computer Science and MS in Management Science, where he conducted machine learning research under Fei-Fei Li and was named a Greylock X Fellow and a Kleiner Perkins Fellow.
