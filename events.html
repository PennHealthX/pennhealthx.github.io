💻 Claude Coding Workshop | 📍 Friday 4/24 12pm Law Auditorium

Albert Opher, Founder of Penn’s Claude Builder Club, will lead an introductory session on getting started with Claude’s Chat and Cowork tools. He will walk through how these tools work and demonstrate practical use cases across research, statistics, data analysis, and more. Bonus: All attendees will receive free access to Claude Pro (a $20/month subscription) for 3 months, as well as free API credits. We highly encourage you to take advantage of this opportunity to learn, build, and continue developing your own tools beyond the session.
